How can we help the homeless in our own homes?

As the gravity of COVID-19 settled into our communities, we realized that there are many in our vicinity who do not have access to basic needs— food, hygiene, and shelter. While staying in home, we realized how having shelter during this pandemic is a privilege. By creating Corody, we want to provide support to our homeless community who are let outside with no place to take refuge.

While we shelter in place, they have no place to shelter

Help us build a kit by sharing items that will provide support to the homeless during this pandemic or by giving us funding we need to purchase the items.We believe in the strength of our community and in our ability to fight for each other. By supporting Corody, you are blooming hope into our future— and without hope we cannot overcome COVID-19.

Step 1

BUILD. We need your support in creating a well rounded kit. Do you have extra pantry goods or sanitary products you can share? Would you consider funding us so that we can purchase necessary supplies?

Step 2

SEND. Send us your support by completing the form below. If you are donating items, we will contact you to come up with a way that facilitates you the most in sending them and respects social distance.

Step 3

SHARE. We promise you that all donations will be used to create the kits. Share with your friends and family so that we can continue support. The more words you disperse, the more kits we disperse!
